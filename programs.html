Residency: Rereading the River
Riverbanks in Yogyakarta · a research program with river-side communities, focusing on flow, waste, and the stories attached to the water.

Workshop: Small Archives of the Neighborhood
Community house, Yogyakarta · a two-day workshop on archiving photos, sounds, and everyday objects as local records.

Discussion: City, Memory, and Temporary Spaces
Art space, Yogyakarta · a public discussion responding to “The Pending City” exhibition and its relation to shifting urban spaces.
